Who We Are?

Established in 1989, ClearMind was born out of a collective vision to create a space where individuals with ADD can thrive. Our founders, brought together their expertise in psychology, education, and advocacy to establish an organization that breaks down barriers and champions the inherent capabilities of those with ADD.

We are a community of ADHD patients who strive to support and empower each other. Our mission is to provide a safe and inclusive space for individuals with ADHD to share their experiences, learn from each other, and grow together.

Our Values

  • Empathy and Understanding
  • Inclusivity and Diversity
  • Growth and Learning
  • Support and Empowerment
  • Helping you understand

What do we do?

  • Education and awareness :

    • We provide comprehensive information about ADHD, helping you better understand its nuances, dispelling myths, and fostering a supportive community that embraces neurodiversity.

  • Skill Development:

    • Our programs focus on building essential skills to navigate the daily challenges associated with ADHD. From time management and organizational strategies to effective communication techniques, we're here to help you develop tools for success.

  • Community Support:

    • Connect with others who share similar experiences in our supportive community. Share insights, seek advice, and celebrate victories big or small with individuals who understand the journey of living with ADHD.

  • Resource Hub:

    • Access a wealth of curated resources, from recommended books and articles to helpful apps and tools. Stay informed and equipped with the latest information to enhance your ADHD journey.

  • Workshops and Events:

    • Engage in interactive workshops and events tailored to the ADHD community. Learn from experts, participate in discussions, and gain practical insights to navigate various aspects of life.

  • Advocacy:

    • We advocate for ADHD awareness and inclusivity in various spheres of life. Together, we work towards reducing stigma and fostering environments that embrace and accommodate neurodiversity.

Our Team

Our team is composed of dedicated volunteers who are passionate about supporting the ADHD community. We come from diverse backgrounds and experiences, but we all share a common goal: to make a positive impact in the lives of ADHD patients.
